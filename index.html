Automated Pinterest Integration for Etsy Sellers

What it does

Etsy Poster automatically creates optimized Pinterest pins from your Etsy product listings, saving hours of manual posting and helping drive traffic to your shop.

Features

  • Automatic pin creation from Etsy products
  • Optimized titles and descriptions for better engagement
  • Direct links to your Etsy shop listings
  • Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Automated workflow with n8n integration

How it works

The application connects to your Etsy shop through their API, retrieves your product information (images, titles, descriptions), and automatically publishes them as pins on your Pinterest account using Pinterest API v5.

Privacy & Security

Your data privacy and security are our top priorities. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to create pins on your behalf. All data transmission happens over secure HTTPS connections.
