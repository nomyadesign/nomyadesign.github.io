Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

Introduction

Nomyadesign ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Etsy Poster application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

OAuth Authentication Data

When you connect your Pinterest account through our App, we collect:

  • Your Pinterest user ID
  • Access tokens necessary to post pins on your behalf
  • Basic profile information (username, display name)

Product Data from Etsy

The App accesses your Etsy shop data to:

  • Retrieve product titles, descriptions, and images
  • Generate optimized Pinterest pins
  • Link pins back to your Etsy listings

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your Pinterest account via OAuth 2.0
  • Automatically create and publish pins from your Etsy products
  • Maintain the connection between your Etsy shop and Pinterest account

Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth tokens are stored securely and encrypted
  • Product data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • All data transmission occurs over secure HTTPS connections

Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with the following third-party services:

Pinterest

  • We use Pinterest API v5 to create pins on your behalf
  • Your use of Pinterest is governed by Pinterest's Privacy Policy
  • We only request the minimum permissions necessary (write access to pins)

Etsy

  • We access your Etsy shop data through your authorized connection
  • Your use of Etsy is governed by Etsy's Privacy Policy

Data Retention

  • OAuth tokens are retained only while your account is active
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Pinterest account settings
  • Upon disconnection, all authentication data is permanently deleted within 7 days

Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time

Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Cookies and Tracking

This App does not use cookies for tracking purposes. OAuth authentication tokens are stored securely in encrypted storage solely for authentication purposes. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising cookies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
